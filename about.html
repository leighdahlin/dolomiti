About Us

Our Founder.

Leigh Dahlin founded Dolomiti because of her passion for the outdoors. She wanted to create a space where people who shared the same passions can find good quality products that they can trust to support them on all their adventures. Her adventurous spirit has taken her all over to world and she wants to help others do the same.

We Created Dolomiti To Inspire the Adventurers.

Our Mission.

We named our company after the mountain range in the Italian Alps because we value the great outdoors and adventure. At the core of our values is helping customers find and enjoy unique experiences in the outdoors, wherever that may be. Providing our customers with the best quality, sustainable products is how we accomplish that.
